About Rangla Punjab

Punjab is known for its vibrant Punjabi Culture. Be it music, cuisines, craft, performing arts, Punjab has excelled. However, certain forms of performing arts are diminishing in the absence of a suitable platform. Rangla Punjab is an initiative taken by Punjab Tourism to promote the unique and hidden talent of Punjab. This unique application and website gives a wonderful platform to the people from around the world to come and showcase their talent in the verticals of performing art like Singing, Dancing and other forms of performing arts. People can come on the web channel and upload their videos showcasing the best of their talent.

Why Rangla Punjab?

Out of all the available platforms today for showcasing of the talent, authenticity and credibility factor is missing in most of them. This is an initiative from Punjab Tourism Promotion Board to promote and uplift the hidden unique talent of Punjab. This platform gives a genuine chance to all talented people where their talent will be showcased and they will get acknowledgment and recognition of their art. There are many chances where the talent uploaded on Rangla Punjab will catch the attention of many famous films and videos producers and Directors which may open the doors of Punjabi and Indian film and music industry for them.

About Videos

Things to know about
your videos?

What kind of videos we can upload?

One can upload videos of their talent which initially includes the areas of singing and Dancing, but will be further extended for other forms of performing arts and crafts. The videos may/may not be supported with the fellow or co-artist or background music or track for singing. Each video will be evaluated by the team of experts. The video will then be uploaded at Rangla Punjab platform.

How your video will be judged?

All the uploaded videos will be duly screened and evaluated by the team of experts. The decision of the evaluation team shall be final and cannot be challenged.

How can you get your videos featured on Punjab Tourism YouTube Channel and Rangla Punjab application?

The user shall have to accept the privacy policy framed for the purpose, at the time of registration. The user will have to register on the application by making an account using his credentials. The account making will be free of charge. Once the account is made, user can go in the upload option, and upload his videos on the platform. It is advisable for the users to write a detailed description along with their videos which will help the panel to get better understanding of their videos. Post evaluation of the videos, the videos which will clear all the criteria of selection will be uploaded on you tube channel of Punjab Tourism and Rangla Punjab application.

What is featured video and how your videos can be considered for featured video section?

A featured video is the one which gets approved by the evaluation panel and makes it way finally to come on the you tube channel of Punjab Tourism and Rangla Punjab application. Clearing all the selection parameters, if video get approved by the evaluation panel, the best video shall be eligible to get featured on you tube channel of Punjab Tourism and Rangla Punjab application. The featured video shall remain on the Rangla Punjab channel till a new video gets approved for placement in the featured section.

How your videos will be approved

For approval to reach the final platform of youtube channel of Punjab Tourism and Rangla Punjab application, each content will be evaluated by the team of experts and the content approved by the committee shall be uploaded on the Rangla Punjab platform.
